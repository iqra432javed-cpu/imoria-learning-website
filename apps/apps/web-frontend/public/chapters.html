Q1 (Board Favorite)

Why do isotopes have identical chemical properties but different physical properties?

Answer: Chemical properties depend on the atomic number (number of electrons and their configuration), which is identical for all isotopes of an element. Physical properties depend upon mass numbers, which are different due to varying numbers of neutrons.
